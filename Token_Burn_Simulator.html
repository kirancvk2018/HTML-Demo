Why determinism saves tokens

No hedging

A scoped, explicit prompt doesn't need the model to caveat, restate assumptions, or cover multiple interpretations "just in case."

Fewer retries

Ambiguous asks are more likely to miss what you actually wanted, forcing a follow-up prompt that re-burns most of the original context.

No clarifying loop

A well-scoped prompt answers its own likely follow-up questions upfront instead of triggering a back-and-forth.

Structured output parses cheap

A defined JSON/table shape is compact to generate and cheap to validate downstream, versus free-form prose that has to be re-read and re-interpreted.
