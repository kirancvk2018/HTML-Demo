Why this matters more in BFSI than almost anywhere else

Most consumer GenAI mistakes are embarrassing. In BFSI, they're reportable. A hallucinated policy clause, an unexplained credit decision, or a leaked account number doesn't just erode trust — it can trigger regulatory findings under frameworks like SR 11-7, GDPR, GLBA, or the EU AI Act, and it can move real money or real risk. Prompt engineering here isn't prompt "crafting" — it's the first line of model risk management: it's where scope, evidence, explainability, and human oversight get encoded before a single output is ever generated. A checklist and analyzer like this doesn't slow delivery down; it's what lets a solution pass model validation, audit, and UAT on the first pass instead of the third.
